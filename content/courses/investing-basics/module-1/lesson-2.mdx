# Risk and return

Higher **expected** returns usually come with more volatility — the possibility of larger swings and drawdowns. There is no free lunch: chasing return means accepting uncertainty.

## Takeaways

- Risk is not just “losing money once” — it is the range of outcomes over time.
- Your time horizon and capacity to stay invested matter when risk shows up.
