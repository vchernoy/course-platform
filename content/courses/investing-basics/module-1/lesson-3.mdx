# Diversification

**Diversification** spreads money across different investments so one bad outcome does not sink the whole plan. It does not remove risk, but it can reduce exposure to any single company, sector, or bet.

## Remember

- Correlations change in stress periods — diversification helps, but it is not magic.
- Keep costs low and avoid overlap when stacking similar funds.
