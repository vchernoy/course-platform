## Math

The compound growth formula in plain symbols: $A = P\cdot(1 + r)^t$.

Display math:

$$
A = P\cdot(1 + r)^t
$$

:::details[Advanced explanation]
Continuous compounding uses $A = Pe^{rt}$. For teaching, discrete periods are easier to visualize—same intuition, different formula family.

:::
